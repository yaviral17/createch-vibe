FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical XR project take? +

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most projects range from 6-16 weeks. Simple AR prototypes can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while complex VR applications may take 3-6 months.

What platforms do you develop for? +

We develop for all major platforms including iOS/Android (AR), Oculus/HTC Vive (VR), HoloLens (MR), and web-based XR solutions using WebXR standards.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including bug fixes, performance optimization, platform updates, and feature enhancements to keep your XR solution current.

Can you help with XR strategy and consulting? +

Absolutely! Our team provides strategic consulting to help you identify the best XR opportunities for your business, from initial concept through implementation and scaling.
